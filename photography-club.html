About Photography Club

Photography Club is one of the most active clubs at Royal College Horana. We capture every special moment of school life — from Sports Meets and Annual Days to Science Exhibitions and Cultural Events. Our members learn professional photography skills, photo editing, and storytelling through images.

Open for students from Grade 6 to 13 • Weekly workshops • Annual Photo Contest
